Species Spotlight

Platinum Arowana

Scleropages formosus

The undisputed monarch of freshwater aquaria. The Platinum Arowana commands extraordinary prices due to its complete lack of pigmentation — a genetic rarity producing scales of pure metallic silver-white. Specimens of verified platinum lineage are among the most sought-after living collectibles in the world.

Min. Tank Size 300 gal
Water Temp 26–29 °C
pH Range 6.5–7.0
Max Size 90 cm
Care Level Expert
Origin SE Asia
