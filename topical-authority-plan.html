Cannibalization Risks

Multiple pages competing for the same keyword intent. Each group below shows overlapping targets and the recommended fix to consolidate authority into a single winning URL.

1. "Discus" Keyword Sprawl
201 non-clustered discus keywords + "discuss fish for sale" cluster + "cobalt blue discus" cluster. Three separate content targets all fighting for "discus fish".
/exotic-fish/discus/ (Category)Hub page
10 individual discus product pagesProducts
"discuss fish for sale" cluster270 vol
"cobalt blue discus" cluster360 vol
Fix: Make /exotic-fish/discus/ the canonical hub for "discus fish" + "discus fish for sale". Each variant (Cobalt Blue, Wild, Pigeon Blood) gets ONE product page targeting its specific variant name. No separate "discus for sale" collection — the category IS the collection. Internal links from all discus products point UP to the category hub.
2. "Catfish" Multi-Cluster Overlap
4 catfish clusters ("colorful catfish" 12.8K, "types of catfish" 11.5K, "blue catfish" 820, "catfish with spikes" 640) + 34 non-clustered catfish keywords. Risk of the category page, blog posts, and species guides all targeting "catfish" head term.

"colorful catfish" cluster12,840 vol
"types of catfish" cluster11,490 vol
"blue catfish for sale" cluster820 vol
Fix: Category page = commercial/transactional ("catfish for sale"). Create ONE blog post for "types of catfish" (informational hub — 11.5K vol). "Colorful catfish" maps to a section within that blog post, NOT a separate page. Each species gets its own product page + species guide. "Blue catfish" maps to a specific product page.
3. "Knife Fish" Fragmentation
5 separate knife fish clusters (clown knife, black ghost, aba aba, brown, glass) + the non-clustered "ghost knife fish" (6.6K) and "knife fish" head term (4.4K). Without a pillar, Google doesn't know which page to rank.

Category page links down to all species; species pages link up to category.

"puffer fish" head term (60.5K) sits in non-clustered. "fresh water puffer fish" cluster (5.2K) and "fahaka puffer for sale" cluster (520) exist. The /exotic-fish/pufferfish/ category page and individual product pages could compete.
